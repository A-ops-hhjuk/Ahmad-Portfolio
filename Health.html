Senior Lecturer and Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (MHPSS) Technical Advisor – Regional MENA

Healthy Nutrition System/Cambridge Education College, Jordan, Amman


My role encompasses designing, planning, and implementing MHPSS programs, courses, materials, and diplomas aligned with human rights, protection frameworks, and global standards. I lead capacity-building initiatives, mentorship, and accredited educational programs that support professionals, international participants, and students across diverse disciplines in the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) region. Additionally, I work within both humanitarian and development frameworks under the Nexus approach, ensuring long-term resilience and sustainable mental health solutions. My work spans all levels of the Inter-Agency Standing Committee (IASC) MHPSS framework (Levels 1, 2, and 3).
