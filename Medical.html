Feb. – May 2013

Clinical Instructor (Advanced Medical Courses)

In addition to my clinical responsibilities, I had the privilege of teaching undergraduate nursing students courses encompassing healthcare, mental health, community health, and management/leadership. I instructed, trained, mentored, and provided direct supervision to students during their clinical rotations, emphasizing the application of theoretical approaches in real-life patient needs and situations. Through practical instruction, I equipped students with the essential skills to navigate and address healthcare challenges, including mental health issues, within diverse community settings. Moreover, I provided guidance in management and leadership, preparing students to effectively coordinate healthcare teams and contribute to quality improvement initiatives. It was fulfilling to contribute to their education, empowering them to provide comprehensive and compassionate care while also developing their managerial and leadership abilities.
