April 2021 – 30th of May 2024

Mental Health Psychosocial Support (MHPSS) Coordinator (equal Senior Manager)

International Medical Corps, Jordan, Amman


The major-specific Responsibilities:

• Program Management: Coordinate and monitor high-quality MHPSS programming including PSS child protection services and PSS GBV services in Jordan, working with the MHPSS technical team, field staff, and program managers. Support the development and implementation of group-based psychosocial programming. Provide clinical supervision to staff and promote integration of MHPSS at the community level.

• Designing and supervising the Child Protection Counseling Program to ensure that all child protection counselors provide high-quality, protection-based psychosocial support (PSS) to children and families experiencing psychological and emotional distress. Additionally, designing and leading community-led initiatives, such as Youth Empowerment Programs that utilize a community-driven project approach to foster resilience and participation.

• Technical Monitoring and Program Quality: Oversee the technical quality of programming and expertise of staff. Provide technical input during proposal development and reviews to improve the quality of final proposals. Collaborate with government health authorities and international agencies to ensure standardized health programming.

• Integration and Coordination: Monitor the integration of mental health and psychosocial support across IMC inter-sectoral programming. Ensure close coordination between the MHPSS department and other departments/sectors.

• Research, Advocacy, and Innovation: Liaise with government structures and local authorities, organizing MHPSS activities and advocating for collaboration. Support the implementation of evidence-based MHPSS programming through research, training, and joint monitoring.

• Additional responsibilities include Psychotropic Medication Management, Human Resources Management, Innovation Learning and Development, and other duties as needed.
