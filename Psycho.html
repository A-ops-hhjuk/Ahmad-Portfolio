Oct. 2015 – Dec 2017

Psychosocial Skills Trainer/ Psychotherapist

The Bright Future for Mental Health (NGOs), Jordan, Amman.


Conduct individual and group counseling, psychotherapy, and psychosocial skills training sessions. Provide primary care services to individuals with psychiatric mental health needs, actively participating in their care planning and treatment process. Collaborate with interdisciplinary team members, including psychiatrists, pharmacists, psychologists, and social workers, to develop and evaluate comprehensive treatment plans. Additionally, deliver psychosocial educational and awareness sessions within the community, with a particular focus on vulnerable populations. As a psychosocial skills trainer, facilitate the development of essential life skills and coping mechanisms to enhance the overall well-being and resilience of individuals and families.
