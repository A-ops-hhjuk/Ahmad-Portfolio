Nov. 2013– Oct. 2016

Medical Claim Center Department at Med-Net Jordan (a member of Munich Health)

Senior Medical Officer


Evaluate and provide medical approval authorizations for various medical services, including in-hospital cases, outpatient services, chronic medication online, dental procedures, and direct claims, ensuring medical necessity and appropriateness. Offer medical advice and consultation to other departments and stakeholders in both private and governmental sectors. Additionally, contribute to the psychosocial well-being of patients by considering the holistic aspects of their care. Compile and update medical data, preparing statistical reports on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis to assess department performance and identify medical trends. Mentor and provide medical education to team members through diverse educational sessions and direct supervision, particularly for new team members. Manage medical approvals and collaborate with internal and external stakeholders to ensure the delivery of the best services for clients, with a focus on promoting psychosocial well-being..
